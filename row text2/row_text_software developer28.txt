Angular UI/UX Developer
Job Description:
As a Front-End Developer, your responsibilities will include designing, developing, testing, and maintaining web application screens while ensuring seamless integration with back-end services. The successful candidate will possess strong critical thinking skills, attention to detail, and the ability to adapt to emerging trends in front-end development.
Responsibilities:
Create wireframes and mockups, then help implement them as interactive web applications.
Develop responsive and visually appealing user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and Angular.
Build custom components in Angular based on functional requirements
Use REST end points to connect Angular front end components with backend
Ensure cross-browser and cross-platform compatibility for optimal user experience.
Implement and maintain design systems and style guides.
Analyze requirements and translate them into technical specifications.
Work closely with back-end developers to integrate front-end designs with back-end services.
Optimize application performance by identifying and resolving bottlenecks.
Conduct thorough testing to ensure the quality and reliability of the final product.
Qualifications:
Authorized to work in the United States without a need for future sponsorship.
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field, or equivalent experience.
At least 4 years of experience as a front-end developer using Angular.
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and front end frameworks Angular.
Strong understanding of responsive design and cross-browser compatibility.
Fluency in the English language for effective communication.
Ability to work full-time (40 hours/week).

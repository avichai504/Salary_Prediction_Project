Overview of the Position

The Incubation Lab is dedicated to data-driven innovation within Commercial Lines Data Science at The Hartford. The lab focuses on value-multiplying our insurance portfolio by evaluating new, cutting-edge technologies, datasets, and insuretech vendors; prototyping predictive models; and supporting The Hartford’s data science teams and their business partners in leveraging data to drive process and product improvements.

The Hartford’s Incubation Lab seeks an energetic and passionate Junior Data Scientist to push forward the design, development, and implementation of solutions modernizing and transforming our operations and business based on vehicle telematics data. This position requires basic knowledge of statistical or probabilistic modeling, data transformation and visualization, and will offer career-accelerating opportunities to build skills and domain expertise in both the business and the craft of data science.

In this role, you will be part of a team working in a fast-paced, open-ended environment to help define how our firm’s data scientists and business teams leverage telematics data to improve our processes and products. Success will depend on your ability to rapidly learn the craft and the business and quickly deliver capabilities and recommendations for partners within the Internet of Things Lab and other business stakeholders.

Responsibilities

Help assess the value of new data sources and analytical techniques to ensure ongoing competitive advantage
Conduct cost-benefit analysis and estimate value-add of data-driven solutions, including third party data sources
Create and evaluate sensor-based features and capabilities using data science and machine learning techniques and state-of-the-art tools and data sources
Engage with technical and business stakeholders to help define and design solution concepts
Support the implementation and delivery of complex technological proofs of concept and implementations

Position Title: Data Scientist I
Department: IT
Reports to: Sr Data Scientist
Location: Remote
Schedule: 35 hours weekly
Salary Grade: $62k-98k
Position Overview
As a Data Scientist I, you will be responsible for working with supervision on coding and testing tools and assets to deliver high quality analytics.
Data Scientist help us maintain and improve our analytics priorities. You will help to support and clarify the organization’s reporting and analytics focuses by exploring data sets for patterns of interest, and contributing to the organization’s evolving data help desk infrastructure needs as they emerge. You will leverage R,Python and/or SQL for EDA, publish tools and findings in RStudio Connect and Tableau, and use CRM and task/project management tools such as Salesforce, Jira and Asana to track progress.

Duties & Responsibilities
Collaborate as a team to identify, assess, and solve business problems
Create quantitative models and identify patterns in data
Create data visualization dashboards to present findings
Maintain and monitor existing data help desk requests to ensure reliability of reports and make recommendations for improvements and enhancements
Use pre-existing tools to discern common concerns from novel road blocks, and communicate or escalate cases to relevant teams
Recognize and adopt best practices in: data privacy, quality communication, and documentation
Qualifications
Hands-on experience with programming languages, such as R, Python or SQL
Comfort translating business requirements into thoughtful research questions
Strong ability to recognize patterns and keen attention to detail
Demonstrated willingness and ability to engage with teams and collectively solve complex problems
Preferred
1-2 years work experience in an analytical role
Bachelor’s degree in Computational Modeling and Data Analytics, Advanced Analytics, Statistics, Business Analytics or a related discipline
Knowledge of and/or interest in using statistical modeling to solve complex problems
Comfort making decisions in a fast-paced environment
